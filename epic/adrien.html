Persona 1

  • Name: Adrien Baca-Garcia
  • Age: 30
  • Ethnicity: Mixed
  • Job: Tech Recruiter
  • Location: Albuquerque, NM
  • Technology: Samsung S9, HP Laptop, is confident in using all technical gadgets, sees benefits of all platforms.
  • Hobbies: Hiking, yoga, code-athons, walking her dog, knits sweaters for her dog
  • Favorite Websites: mdn.com, github.com, etsy.com
  • Behaviors: Busy with work and does not like to "party" on the weekends. Is career driven right now. Does not like being hit on at bars by sleazy guys.
  • Attitudes: Open to new experiences, ready to take a chance and date someone she may have not otherwise considered.
  • Frustrations: She is frustrated with the hook up culture of the current dating apps, they don't allow for a more meaningful connection within the app.

User Story

Adrien feels like her life is fulfilled in all aspects. Her career is on point, her dog is a great companion and she is ready for a boo thang. She's fed up with dating sites promote hook ups. She wants to have a real connection and fall in love.

Use Case

Title: Looking for a boyfriend

Description: Adrien gets on the site to find love

Name of the User and Their Role: Adrien Baca-Garcia is the potential love match

Usage Preconditions: Internet capable device, must be logged into account

Usage Post-conditions: Website matches Adrien with her possible love interest

Interaction Flow:

  • Adrien enters the URL on her laptop using Chrome browser.
  • Server receives requested site and returns landing page of the site.
  • Adrien is logged in from previous session and wants to check her likes list.
  • She clicks on likes.
  • Server responds with the list of likes.
  • She clicks the first user with the heart icon.
  • Server responds with the users profile.
  • She notices the message box and sends a message.
  • Server accepts request and generates email to send to matched user.
  • She clicks on her home button to view other users.
  • Server responds and displays her home page showing all the users.
  • She clicks on the user who catches her eye.
  • Server responds with the users profile.
  • She reads the profile and decides to tap on the heart to indicate she likes him.
  • Server records new instance in like table.
  • She's done browsing, she closes the window.

Frequency of Use: Three to four times a week.
