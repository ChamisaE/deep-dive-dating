  • loverEmail
  • loverGender
  • loverHandle

Personality

  • personalityId (primary key)
  • personalityLoverId (foreign key)

Deal Breaker

  • dealBreakerId (primary key)
  • dealBreakerLoverId (foreign key)

Match

  • matchFromLoverId (foreign key)
  • matchToLoverId (foreign key)

Relationships

  • One Lover has one Deal Breaker
  • One Lover can have many Personalities
  • One Lover can have many Matches
